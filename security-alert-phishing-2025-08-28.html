Security Alert - Phishing Email Warning:

Dear all,

If you received these email messages, please don't open the attachment and contact the IT department immediately.

What to do if you receive suspicious emails:

• Do NOT open any attachments

• Do NOT click on any links

• Do NOT reply to the email

• Forward the email to IT Department immediately

Contact IT Support immediately

Security Guidelines:

  • Always verify the sender before opening emails
  • Be suspicious of unexpected attachments or urgent requests
  • When in doubt, contact IT Security team immediately
  • Report any suspicious activity to IT Department immediately

إرشادات الأمان:

  • تحقق دائماً من المرسل قبل فتح الرسائل الإلكترونية
  • كن مشكوكاً في المرفقات غير المتوقعة أو الطلبات العاجلة
  • عند الشك، اتصل بفريق أمن تقنية المعلومات فوراً

Critical Security Reminders:

• Never open attachments from unknown or suspicious senders

• Do not click on suspicious links in emails

• Always verify unusual requests by contacting the sender directly

If you suspect a phishing email, contact IT Security immediately

Your vigilance helps protect our entire organization.
